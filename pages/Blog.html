Sumba, NTT Indonesia

Sumba Island is a suitable place for tourists who want to feel the beauty of unspoiled nature and strong traditional culture.

Get To Know Sumba

Sumba Island is an island located in the province of East Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia. The history of the island of Sumba records that this island has a very rich and unique culture, and has been home to various ethnic groups since prehistoric times.

Sumba Island is known as an island that has many strong cultures and traditions. One of the well-known Sumba culture is the Pasola ceremony, which is a traditional ceremony performed by the Sumba tribe in which they throw spears at each other in commemoration of the rice harvest.

Apart from that, Sumba Island also has a traditional Sumba house called Mbaru Niang, which was built using natural materials such as bamboo and wood.

Here are some things that can be found on the island of Sumba :

  1. Sumba Island has very beautiful beaches with white sand and clear sea water such as Weekuri Beach, Bwanna Beach and Karoso Beach.
  2. The Sumba tribe is known for its unique customs and culture, such as the Pasola traditional ceremony, and the Sumba traditional house which is called the Marapu traditional house.
  3. Lapopu Waterfall: This waterfall is located in West Sumba Regency and has stunning natural beauty with a 25 meter high waterfall and a clear pool.
  4. In addition, Sumba Island also offers various other tourist activities such as trekking, camping and diving.

You Must Know Wakatobi

The Wakatobi Islands are one of the famous marine tourism destinations in Indonesia because they have spectacular underwater natural beauty.

You Must Know Wakatobi

Wakatobi is an archipelago in Southeast Sulawesi, Indonesia which consists of four main islands, namely Wangi-wangi, Kaledupa, Tomia, and Binongko. These islands are a very attractive place for tourists who want to enjoy the spectacular underwater beauty and also want to explore the local culture and culinary specialties of the area.

Here are some of the reasons why you should visit Wakatobi:

  1. Wakatobi has beautiful and well-preserved coral reefs, making it one of the best diving locations in Indonesia. These coral reefs are home to various types of fish and marine life.
  2. Besides the coral reefs, Wakatobi also has various types of marine life such as sharks, dolphins, turtles, and more. Tourists can enjoy the beauty of the underwater world through snorkeling or diving.
  3. Wakatobi has four main islands, each with its own unique attractions. Wangi-wangi has stunning beaches, while Tomia is known as a diver's paradise.
  4. In addition to its natural beauty, Wakatobi also has a rich and unique local culture, including customs, traditional arts, and handicrafts.
  5. Wakatobi is an important place for marine conservation due to its high species diversity. There are more than 750 species of fish and 850 species of coral reefs and octopuses.
  6. Wakatobi has provided adequate tourist facilities, such as lodging, restaurants and transportation services, so that tourists can enjoy their holidays comfortably.

Fun Fact Ubud Bali, Indonesia

This most popular tourist spot in Bali has a unique name, And its heaven for foodies. The city has many restaurants serving delicious Balinese cuisine, as well as a variety of international cuisines.

Fun Fact Ubud Bali, Indonesia

Ubud is a small town in Gianyar, Bali, Indonesia. This city is known for its rich traditional Balinese culture and arts, as well as its stunning natural beauty.

The name Ubud comes from the word "ubad", which in Balinese means medicine or herbal medicine. This is related to the existence of forests and medicinal plants in this area.

Ubud is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Bali. The city has many art galleries, craft shops, restaurants and spas. In addition, around Ubud there are many interesting natural tourist attractions, such as beautiful terraced rice fields, the amazing Ayung river, and a unique monkey forest.

Ubud, Bali has its own uniqueness that makes this city one of the most popular tourist attractions in Bali. Here 3 of the uniqueness of Ubud Bali:

  1. Rich Balinese Art and Culture.

    Ubud is famous for its rich and unique Balinese art and culture. The city has been the center of Balinese artistic and cultural activities since the early 20th century, and artists and painters from all over the world often come here to seek inspiration.

  2. Calm and Peaceful Place.

    Ubud has a calm and peaceful atmosphere, in contrast to the noise and hustle of big cities in Bali such as Kuta or Seminyak. This city is located in the middle of the mountains, so it has cool and fresh air.

  3. Famous Place of Meditation and Yoga.

    Ubud is also famous as a place for meditation and yoga which is popular in Bali. The city has many meditation and yoga centers offering different classes, from those suitable for beginners to more challenging ones.
